Un-Boxed

Un-Boxed is a website that serves as a community for out-of-the-box thinkers to post information about products, news, events, and media around the topic of innovation. My inspiration for this project stems from my own inclination to find creative solutions to problems through uncoventional approaches. Through this site, other creative thinkers can come together to share their their own innovative discoveries with a wider audience of likeminded peers.

Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Google Books API

My Booklist App

My Book List is a simple app that can be used by anyone who wants to keep track of the books the want to read and the books they have finished reading. However, as a former elementary teacher, my original inspiration was for emerging readers to use this app as a tool to be prepared for idependent reading time and to make sure they persevere to finish one book before moving on to the next one.
